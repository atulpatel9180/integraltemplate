About

Integral University was established in 2004. The University is approved by the University Grants Commission under Sections 2(f) and 12B of the UGC Act, 1956, MCI, PCI, BCI, INC, CoA, NCTE, UPSMF, IAP & DEB. Member of AIU. Accredited by NAAC. Courses Accredited by NBA. Recognized as a SCIENTIFIC AND INDUSTRIAL RESEARCH ORGANIZATION (SIRO) by the Dept. of Scientific and Industrial Research (DSIR), Ministry of Science & Technology.
